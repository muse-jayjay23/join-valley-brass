Why Join Valley Brass?

Learn a new skill

Never had the chance to learn an instrument? Or, did you play back in the day and want to rekindle your love in music? Learning an instrument is great for the mind and helps improve focus and resilience.

Enjoy our rehearsal rooms

We have multiple rooms within the building, a tea room, pool room, games room as well as outside fenced play areas for sport.
